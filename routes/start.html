Helping healthcare to, with minimal effort, provide relevant and adapted information to patients.

Patient information

After or during medical treatment, e.g. after surgery or during cancer treatment, patients get a lot of information about their treatment from their doctor and from other health care professionals. But most of this information is given orally and can be hard to remember for a patient when coming home. It is therefore crucial, and in some cases required by the authorities, that written information about the treatment is provided to the patient.

Healthcare professionals do usually not have the time and resources to produce relevant and adapted written information and the written information provided to the patient is thus usually either very general or poor or even lacking.

Our solution

Our solution to this problem is our applications Patientbesked and Min vårdplan. With these applications a healthcare professional, usually a nurse, can produce relevant and reviewed information, adapted to the patient, in around 30 seconds.

Both applications are based on a template engine in which templated information is stored in a hirearcial structure. The template information consists of medical information but also contact information, where to get more information, treatment timelines etc.

Patientbesked is used to provide information after institutional and non-institutional care, e.g. after surgery, while Min vårdplan is used to produce individual care plans during cancer treatment.

Patientbesked

patientbesked.se

Patientbesked is used to generate patient information after institutional and non-institutional care.

The application is based on a template engine where the templates are stored in a hierarchial structure where the top level is diagnosis group.

The basic template information includes medical information about the diagnosis and the treatment as well as contact information.

But the application is very flexible and the type of template information can be customized, i.e. new template entities can be added. This may include e.g. information about self care, information about return visit etc.

Each clinic has its own set of templates and the medical template information is created and reviewed by doctors at the clinic.

When creating a patient information a nurse picks and chooses among the templates and a relevant and personally adapted information is produced in around 30 seconds.

Patientbesked has been used since 2012 at the following hospitals:
Länssjukhuset i Kalmar, Hallands sjukhus, Skånes universitetessjukhus, Blekingesjukhuset, Centrallasarettet Växjö.

During 2016 almost 9000 patients were provided with information generated by Patientbesked.

Min vårdplan

minvardplan.se

Min vårdplan is used to create individual care plans for patients undergoing cancer treatment. Care plans can be generated both for the investigation phase and the treatment phase and the contents of the created care plans follow the guidelines given by SKL and RCC.

The application is based on the well tested template engine developed for Patientbesked.

The template information includes medical information, various contact information and a timeline for the planned treatment.

Since mid January 2016 a pilot project is running at Blekingesjukhuset where Min vårdplan is used to create care plans for all their colorectal cancer patients.
