What we do

The patient information challenge

After or during medical treatment, e.g. after surgery or during cancer treatment, patients get a lot of information about their treatment from their doctor and from other healthcare professionals. But most of this information is given verbally and can be hard to remember for a patient when at home. It is therefore crucial, and in some cases required by the authorities, that written information about the treatment is provided to the patient.

However, healthcare professionals usually do not have the time and resources to produce relevant and adapted written information. If written information is at all provided, it is often either sparse or very general.

Our solution

Our solution to this problem is our applications Patientbesked and Min vårdplan. With these applications, a healthcare professional, usually a nurse, can produce relevant and reviewed information, adapted to the individual case, with a minimum workload.

Both applications are based on a template engine, developed by Postdoc. The template entities are interlinked, which enables a nurse to create a patient information in around 30 seconds.

The patient receives the information printed in paper form and can also access it online.

Patientbesked is used to provide information after institutional and non-institutional care, e.g. after surgery, while Min vårdplan is used to produce individual care plans during cancer treatment.

Patientbesked

patientbesked.se

Patientbesked is used to generate patient information after institutional and non-institutional care.

The application is based on a template engine where the template entities are interlinked. The basic template entities include medical information about the diagnosis and various contact information. However, the template engine is very flexible and can be customized at each clinic. Template entities like self care, information about return visits etc. can be added and linked to the diagnosis entity.

Each clinic has its own set of templates and the template information is created and maintained by medical expertise at the clinic.

When creating a patient information, a nurse can pick and choose among the templates, and combine them into a relevant information, adapted for the specific case, in around 30 seconds.

Patientbesked has been in use since 2012 at the following hospitals:
Länssjukhuset i Kalmar, Hallands sjukhus, Skånes universitetssjukhus, Blekingesjukhuset and Centrallasarettet Växjö.

During 2016 almost 9000 patients were provided with information generated by Patientbesked.

Min vårdplan

minvardplan.se

Min vårdplan is used to create individual care plans for patients undergoing cancer treatment. Care plans can be generated both for the investigation phase and the treatment phase, and the contents of the created care plans follow the guidelines given by SKL and RCC.

The application is based on the template engine developed for Patientbesked and medical expertise at each clinic create and maintain the template information. The information includes medical information, various contact information and a timeline for the planned treatment.

The created care plans are living documents and can be updated at any time when the conditions change. Patients can access their care plans online and also update certain areas concering their health status. This provides healthcare with important information about the patient's health condition.

Since mid January 2016 a pilot project is running at Blekingesjukhuset, where Min vårdplan is used to create care plans for cancer patients. Initially all colorectal cancer patients at the hospital will be included in the pilot project. This will subsequently be extended to other cancer diagnoses.
